About AMIGOS

The AMIGOS Game Development Club is Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Chennai's first club to offer a space for students with a passion for gaming and game development to explore the exciting world of game development. We're not just about playing games – we're about building them! Unleash your creativity: Turn your wildest gaming ideas into reality. Design immersive worlds, craft compelling characters, and code the mechanics that bring your vision to life. Learn from the best: Gain hands-on experience with different game engines, programming languages, and design tools. Our skilled mentors will guide you through the process, from concept to completion. Collaborate and grow: Team up with fellow gamers and programmers to build amazing projects together. Share your knowledge, learn from each other, and forge friendships that last beyond the screen. Get noticed: Showcase your creations at game jams, hackathons, and other events. Build your portfolio, expand your network, and pave the way for your future in the gaming industry. AMIGOS is more than just a club – it's a community. We believe that everyone has the potential to be a game developer, regardless of experience or skill level. Whether you're a seasoned programmer or just starting out, we welcome you with open arms and a shared passion for creating games.

Activities & Projects

Map Design in Unity

Designers unleashed their creativity by sculpting immersive post-apocalyptic landscapes. Utilizing Unity's powerful tools, participants crafted desolate wastelands, crumbling cities, and hidden sanctuaries, each with its own unique story to tell.

3D Modeling using Blender

The theme of lighthouses inspired artists to create stunning 3D models, blending elements of futuristic architecture with traditional maritime motifs. Entries ranged from towering beacons amidst stormy seas to solitary structures perched on rugged cliffs, serving as beacons of hope in dark, dystopian worlds.

Script Writing

Writers showcased their narrative prowess by crafting compelling stories set in dystopian futures. Themes of technological advancements, societal upheavals, and human resilience dominated the entries, offering glimpses into immersive cyberpunk worlds.

Character Sketching

Artists brought cyberpunk characters to life with their creative interpretations. From augmented humans to rogue hackers, the entries showcased a rich tapestry of cybernetic enhancements, neon-lit cityscapes, and gritty personalities.

Top-Down Turmoil

Join the intense battle in a world plagued by chaos. In "Top-Down Turmoil," players will navigate through challenging mazes filled with enemies, armed with powerful weapons and strategic skills. Engage in thrilling shootouts, dodge enemy bullet, and conquer levels filled challenging opponents.

Eternal Kombat

Prepare to enter the ultimate battle arena in "Eternal Kombat: Realm of Legends," a 2D fighting game that combines lightning-fast combat, brutal finishing moves, and an epic storyline.

Missile Mayhem

Prepare for an adrenaline-fueled escape in "Missile Mayhem." Navigate through a maze of deadly missiles, each one a ticking time bomb waiting to explode. Swiftly maneuver your way through tight spaces.

Jetstream Juggernaut

Gear up for a futuristic battle in "Jetstream Juggernaut." Equip your jet pack, load your shotgun, and holster your pistol as you soar through breathtaking landscapes. Engage in heart-pounding aerial combat, blasting enemies and dodging incoming fire.

Street Surfer

Urban Escape Embark on a high-octane adventure in "Street Surfer." Join a fearless skater escaping through bustling city streets, evading obstacles, and performing jaw-dropping stunts. Race against time, and collect power-ups to enhance your skills.

Tasks

Welcome to Valhav, where shadows dance upon once-vibrant streets. Aye, Adventurer, our town wasn't always like this. It was once a place of joy, laughter, and bustling life, where merchant's calls echoed and children's giggles filled the air. But now, a darkness we call the Fold, born of some evil power, shrouds our once resplendant home. Many moons have passed since the Fold draped itself over our rooftops, leaving our souls restless and our skies in a state of eternal gloom. The townsfolk whisper of eerie phenomena and everlasting chilling murmurs and screeches. Now, you stand at our gates, marked by fate, I know. Could you be the Light Bringer the prophet babbled about in his last breath? The harbinger of hope destined to bring light back to our streets and restore our town to its former vibrance? Will you answer our silent plea and embark on a quest to cast off the Fold and free Valhav from its suffocating grip?

  • Quest 1: The Hero's Logbook - Objective: Establish a GitHub repository as the designated space for uploading your works.

  • Quest 2: Cartographer's - Objective: Explore the town and its surrounding areas. Pick a place of interest, such as a murky marsh or a town square, recreate it as a map using the Unity Engine. Also give it a suitable name.

  • Quest 3: The Blacksmith's Forge - Objective: Pay the local blacksmith a visit and have him craft equipment on Blender, a 3D creation suite, for you. It could be a simple wooden chair for you to rest on or even a sword.

  • Quest 4: Traveler's Sketchbook - Objective: Seek out Valhav's diverse inhabitants. Create a sketch, digital or on paper, of yourself - the main character, the townsfolk, or even malevolent entities lurking in the shadows. Ensure the character or evil entity is named appropriately

  • Quest 5: Words of the Prophet - Objective: Create your own tale and lore write a questline that is concise and at least 500 words long, including dialogues. Make use of characters, objects, and settings that fit the challenge's theme. Give your questline a suitable name.
